Scrambled Eggs

Description

These scrambled eggs are the creamiest, best version!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • (Optional) Milk or heavy cream

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, break the eggs and add the milk
  2. Whisk until smooth and combined, with no streaksw of egg whites remaining
  3. Add salt to taste
  4. Heat a skillet (preferably nonstick) and melt butter until bubbling
  5. Pour egg mixture into skillet
  6. Take a spatula and continuously stir and agitate eggs
  7. Stir until eggs are slightly undercooked, then remove pan from head and transer eggs to a plate
  8. Add pepper to taste
